Summary

A man wearing pink shirt and blood stain on his shirt was walking abnormly like he had pain in his knees to his house. A girl named mansi who was his sister in law opens the door and her laughter dropped on seeing the man and she walks back to sit on the sofa where her boyfriend was present.

The man continued that I was dead and someone had tried to kill him and where is the police and hadn’t they called the police yet and why is everything normal. Mansi asks if he is drunk in the morning and her boyfriend agrees with her and says that the man indeed looked drunk.

The man was irritated with the presence of his sister in law's boyfriend and asked if he was there all night to which the girl said that he is her boyfriend and she can call him whenever she likes.

The man reminded her that it is not her house and doesn't care what the man has gone through. She asks the man to not start any drama.

He tells her that this is no drama and he was dead actually.

She asks why would she believe him as he is standing in from of him in one piece.

He asks if she doesn’t see, the blood stain and the wound on his forehead.

The girls laughs off this issue by calling him a drama queen.

He asks her to look at the condition of his clothes and She tells him that his clothes are good but has bad sense of fashion. And as far as the wound is considered she suggested her to take a painkiller. She was rude to him and didn’t care about his condition.

The man checks himself at the mirror to assure that the wound on his forehead and condition of his clothes and runs to mansi to tell her only to find her paying no heed to him.

He tells her that someone had put him in a plastic bag and threw him in the pond.

His wife believes that he is complaining because he doesn’t like mansi’s boyfriend neeraj. He tries to convince his wife that he was injured and shows her his wounds, his wife reacts rudely and tells him to stop it and mansi will sooner or later break up with neeraj and then he can marry her off with a rich man.

The man asks his wife if she can’t see the blood stains on his shirt to which she said no there is no blood stain and that he is looking handsome.

Mansi tells her sister that she is grateful that the guard told her that he saw her brother in law climbing out of the canal otherwise they would have been shocked to see him alive this morning and then she goes out and calls the guard.

When Mamta got closer to check on his husband if he is dead and shocked to see her husband waking up and eating poha again.
