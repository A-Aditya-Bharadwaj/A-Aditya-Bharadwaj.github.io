Name of the Event:

dubki Play

Summary

A female constable Ganga was making tea when she gets a call from the male SHO, Durgesh Mishra and she says that she is coming. On reaching the police station, she was trying to talk to him about a case when Durgesh started passing derogatory comments on her. A family of mother, father and a girl child visits the police station along with the girl’s teacher and the parents complain about the teacher harassing the girl , Ganga slaps the teacher and tells him that she will deal with him once she files the case.

A gangster named Balum Mafia visits the police station on Durgesh’s request and files an acknowledgement about a crime he has committed. The report was written by Ganga . The gangster was also seen passing lewd comments on Ganga.

Durgesh visits Ganga’s house at night and harasses Ganga. Ganga is seen traumatized after the incident and visits an IPS officer to discuss the case. The IPS officer dismisses her plea of taking action against Durgesh Mishra as he feels it is a pity case. Later, the IPS officer calls Durgesh and tells him to control the Balum mafia.

Durgesh and Balum Mafia were going somewhere and Durgesh said that he is taking him to another jail but stopped the car in the middle where Durgesh says that he will kill the gangster but before Durgesh could reach to his gun Balum shoots him and then goes to meet Ganga. It was Ganga who had informed Balum about a plan encounter and gave Balum a gun to protect himself. Later when Balum meets Ganga, she shoots and kills him and takes bath in the river.
